💖 New Mom Advice: Babywearing Basics

💡 Get the fit right. Always make sure your baby is in an "M" position, knees higher than bum, facing in until ~6 months.

💡 Check baby's breathing. Keep their chin off their chest, airways visible, and face close enough to kiss.

💡 Trust your instincts. Every baby is different. What works for one mom might not work for you, and that's okay.

💡 Keep calm and carry on. It might take a few tries to get it right. Take your time. You're learning together.
